Context Management

This section provides guidelines regarding the use of the SOCIETIES Context Management facilities by third party context-aware services. The Context Management (CM) component block acts as an intermediate layer between context-aware services and the sources of context information.

Setup

Add the following dependencies to your pom.xml file:

<dependency> <groupId>org.societies.api.external</groupId> <artifactId>societies-api</artifactId> <version>${release.version}</version> <scope>provided</scope> </dependency>

And the following configuration on your bundle-context-osgi.xml

<osgi:reference id="ctxBroker" interface="org.societies.api.context.broker.ICtxBroker" />
